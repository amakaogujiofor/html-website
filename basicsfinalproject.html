“A pineapple has pointy defenses but it’s still sweet.
Be a pineapple.” – Unknown

Who wouldn't love pineapples? This spicy little elixir made with fresh ginger and yummy tropical pineapples is nature's potion of anti-inflammatory, tommy soothing and immune-boosting wonders that packs a serious punch.
You should always go for it when your digestive system needs a little comfort. Just a cold glass of beautifully juiced pineapples and flaming hot ginger makes a bad day turn out super great.

Give it a Shot. Here's an easy recipe to get you going!!

  • 🍍Prep fruit and roots. Peel and chop pineapple, ginger, turmeric and lime.
  • 🍍Juice fruit and roots. Push pineapple, ginger, turmeric and lime one at a time through a high-powered juicer.
  • 🍍Stir and Enjoy. Stir the juice with a wooden spoon to blend well.
  • 🍍Pour 1/4 glass full of juice, 1/4 glass full of crushed ice, and fill the rest of the glass with coconut water or distilled water.
  • 🍍Garnish with lime slices and fresh mint.
